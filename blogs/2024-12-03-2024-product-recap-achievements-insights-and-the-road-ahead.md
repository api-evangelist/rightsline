This initiative engaged with customers to better understand how they interact with our platform and uncover opportunities for future UX enhancements.</p>
</div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div><div class="vc_row row-container" id="row-unique-47"><div class="row single-top-padding no-bottom-padding single-h-padding limit-width row-parent"><div class="wpb_row row-inner"><div class="wpb_column pos-top pos-center align_left column_parent col-lg-12 one-internal-gutter"><div class="uncol style-light"><div class="uncoltable"><div class="uncell"><div class="uncont no-block-padding col-custom-width"><div class="vc_custom_heading_wrap "><div class="heading-text el-text"><h5 class="h5"><span>Exciting New Features</span></h5></div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="uncode_text_column"><p>2024 brought a suite of powerful tools and features which included:</p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><strong>Component List Pages for Efficient Data Organization</strong>—originating from our 2023 Strategic Partner Program, this feature drives data management efficiencies, enabling users to organize and access information quickly and intuitively. It has since become one of our most widely used new tools!</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><strong>Configuration Comparison Tool for Streamlined System Customizations</strong>—simplifies system customizations by enabling users to quickly and confidently confirm configuration changes across environments.</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><strong>Audit History Navigator for Detailed Tracking &amp; Transparency</strong>—centralizes audit data across records, allowing users to track changes to fields,  lifecycle stages, relationships, and to easily identify who made changes and when they occurred.  This is the first of several data navigator tools that are in the works.</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><strong>New Tools for Avails and Conflict Reporting</strong>—introduced over a dozen Audit and Conflict Engine tools, including an enhanced UI for rights history, new conflict types, preferences administration, export options, and a streamlined resolution dashboard for efficient conflict management.</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><strong>Enhanced Accounting, Currency, and Allocation Capabilities</strong>—features new allocation methods, reusable percentage-based allocation models, dynamic GL assignments, and improved currency conversion controls.</li>
</ul>

These insights are helping shape our product roadmap with a laser focus on improving productivity, expanding AI-enabled features, and enhancing our financial capabilities. Here are a few of the top suggestions we’re considering as we shape the future of our roadmap:</p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;">Enhancing <strong>productivity tools</strong> like Task Management, more bulk tools, and customizable dashboards.</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;">Harnessing <strong>AI/ML</strong> for smarter contract ingestion, natural language interfaces, financial anomaly detection, and recommendations to streamline daily operations.</li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;">Empowering users with <strong>self-service portals</strong> to reduce inquiries and manual tasks by enabling features like sales report uploads and expanded dashboards for external users.</li>
</ul>
