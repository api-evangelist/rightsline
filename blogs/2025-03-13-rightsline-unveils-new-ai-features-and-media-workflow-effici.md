<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“Rightsline is driving the future of rights and royalties management with innovations that supercharge IP commerce workflows,” said Patrick Arkeveld, CEO of Rightsline. “From AI-driven tools to intuitive reporting and a streamlined portal, we’re committed to delivering game-changing, smarter, and more efficient solutions that empower our customers to move at the speed of today’s media landscape.”</span></p>
</div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div><div class="vc_row row-container" id="row-unique-34"><div class="row single-top-padding no-bottom-padding single-h-padding limit-width row-parent"><div class="wpb_row row-inner"><div class="wpb_column pos-top pos-center align_left column_parent col-lg-12 one-internal-gutter"><div class="uncol style-light"><div class="uncoltable"><div class="uncell"><div class="uncont no-block-padding col-custom-width"><div class="vc_custom_heading_wrap "><div class="heading-text el-text"><h5 class="h5"><span>AI-Driven Rights &amp; Royalties Innovation</span></h5></div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="uncode_text_column"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rightsline is pushing the boundaries of AI and machine learning to transform rights and royalties workflows. Highlights include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Intelligent Contract Extraction</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Rightsline’s AI-powered Optical Character Recognition (OCR) rapidly ingests long-form contracts (PDFs, Word documents, emails), reducing data entry time from hours to minutes. Key contract details are automatically extracted and mapped to Rightsline’s data model, with AI chat functionality helping validate and resolve discrepancies for improved accuracy.</span></li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Deal Comparison &amp; Insights</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Rightsline’s AI-powered Deal Comparison feature allows users to compare manually entered data with AI-extracted data, identifying discrepancies and improving accuracy. The Deal Insights tool provides clear, human-readable responses from the entire rights database, while the AI contract assistant helps analyze contracts and metadata with customizable outputs in style, tone, and length.</span></li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Actionable Avails</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Rightsline’s Avails AI simplifies availability searches with natural language processing, turning complex data into actionable sales insights. Rights Explorer chat allows for easy analysis of availability results, providing clear summaries of key details, including partial availability and contributing rights, with quick, digestible insights.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“AI and machine learning are transforming how companies manage rights and royalties, and Rightsline is at the forefront of this change,” said Matt Bricker, CPTO and Technical Co-Founder of Rightsline. “Our AI-powered tools are designed to reduce friction, increase productivity, and help our customers make faster, smarter decisions. By enabling users to interact and validate data on the fly, we’re helping our customers gain real-time insights and navigate the complexities of today’s media workflows with greater speed and accuracy.”</span></p>

Key features include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Dynamic Visualizations</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Interactive charts, such as amortization curves, give customers more control and clarity when managing their rights and royalties data.</span></li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Data Integration</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Combine app data with external spreadsheets for deeper insights and comparisons.</span></li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Flexible Scheduling &amp; Export Options</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Schedule reports and export to destinations like Google Drive, S3, or email.</span></li>
<li style="margin-left: 40px; padding-bottom: 10px; line-height: 1.5;"><b>Version History</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Track changes in report designs over time for better control.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>
